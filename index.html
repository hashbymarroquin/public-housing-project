LA Data Project

In this website, clicking the "GET DATA" button will enable you to view data on public housing projects. The information explains the name of each plublic housing projects , where it is located, how many acres each public housing area is taking up, how many occupied units there are with their rate and residents, how much average rent , and the monthly income of each family. For example, the information can be used if searching for a place to live .
